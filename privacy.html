Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 01, 2025

Important: PushNotifyApp is designed for enterprise and business use. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our notification management application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

  • Device Identifier: We collect your device's unique identifier (UDID) to route notifications to your specific device
  • App Version: We collect your app version and build number for compatibility and support purposes
  • iOS Version: We collect your iOS version to ensure proper functionality

1.2 Notification Data

  • Notification Content: We temporarily store notification titles, messages, and metadata sent to your device
  • Delivery Status: We track whether notifications were successfully delivered to your device
  • Read Status: We track whether you have read or acknowledged notifications
  • Interaction Data: We collect data about your interactions with notifications (taps, swipes, actions)

1.3 Usage Analytics

  • App Usage: We collect information about how you use the app (screen views, feature usage)
  • Performance Data: We collect crash reports and performance metrics to improve app stability
  • Session Duration: We track how long you use the app for analytics purposes

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 OneSignal Push Notification Service

We use OneSignal to deliver push notifications to your device. OneSignal may collect:

  • Device token for push notification delivery
  • App usage and engagement metrics
  • Device and browser information

OneSignal's privacy policy: https://onesignal.com/privacy_policy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Notification Delivery: To send push notifications to your registered device
  • App Functionality: To provide core app features like notification management and stream subscriptions
  • Performance Improvement: To analyze app usage and improve performance and user experience
  • Support: To provide technical support and troubleshoot issues
  • Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized access or misuse

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Notification Data: Stored for 30 days, then automatically deleted
  • Device Registration: Stored until you uninstall the app or request deletion
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for up to 2 years
  • Location: Data is stored on secure servers in the United States

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With OneSignal for push notification delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions (with notice)

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (note: this may affect app functionality)
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications through iOS settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access to data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication mechanisms

8. Children's Privacy

PushNotifyApp is designed for business and enterprise use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the app (for significant changes)
